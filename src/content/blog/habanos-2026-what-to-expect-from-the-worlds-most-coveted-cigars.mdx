The humidor is never truly full for a true cigar aficionado. Even as we savor the latest releases from Cuba, our eyes are already turning to the horizon—to the promise of new Habanos that will soon grace our collections. While the XXVI Festival del Habano is still months away, the whispers and official announcements have already set the stage for what promises to be an extraordinary year: **2026 will be dominated by the 60th Anniversary of Cohiba, alongside special attention to H. Upmann and Trinidad.**

Let's dive into what we can likely expect from Habanos S.A. in 2026.

### The Crown Jewel: Cohiba's 60th Anniversary

This is, without a doubt, the main event. Cohiba, the legendary brand born in 1966 as Fidel Castro's personal blend, will celebrate its 60th anniversary. Every decade, Cohiba marks its milestones with truly spectacular and often record-breaking releases.

**What to expect from Cohiba in 2026:**

- **A New Commemorative Humidor:** Expect an ultra-exclusive, exquisitely crafted humidor, likely limited to a few hundred numbered pieces worldwide. These humidors typically contain a unique vitola (size and shape) never before seen in the Cohiba portfolio, or a special version of an existing one. Think along the lines of the 50 Aniversario humidor, which contained the groundbreaking 60 ring gauge cigar.
- **A "Regular" Production 60th Anniversary Cigar:** Beyond the humidor, Habanos usually releases a more accessible (though still highly premium and limited) anniversary cigar, often presented in special boxes. This could be a new, larger ring gauge cigar added temporarily to the Cohiba Línea Clásica or Behike lines, or a completely new sub-series.
- **Behike Expansion?** While the Behike BHK 58 was announced for 2025's 15th anniversary, it wouldn't be out of character for Cohiba's 60th to also touch upon the Behike line, perhaps with another limited or unique vitola.
- **Tribute to El Laguito:** Expect significant emphasis on the El Laguito factory, the historic home of Cohiba, and the master rollers (Torcedores) who craft these iconic cigars.

### Spotlight Brands: H. Upmann & Trinidad

While Cohiba will steal the show, Habanos S.A. always features other brands prominently at the Festival. For 2026, **H. Upmann and Trinidad** will share the spotlight. This means we can anticipate exciting new additions to their portfolios:

- **H. Upmann:** Known for its refined, light-to-medium bodied profile, H. Upmann often introduces elegant, sometimes larger, vitolas. We might see:**New Regular Production Vitolas:** Perhaps an addition to the Magnum series or a unique format.
- **Edición Limitada or Regional Edition:** H. Upmann is a prime candidate for a global Edición Limitada or several regional editions in different territories, offering unique sizes with aged tobacco.
- **Special Packaging:** Keep an eye out for special commemorative boxes celebrating the brand's heritage.

**Trinidad:** This brand has seen a significant resurgence and expansion in recent years, moving beyond its traditional "pigtail" cap and becoming a major player. For 2026, we could expect:

- **Further Portfolio Expansion:** New regular production sizes that continue to build out the brand's offerings, possibly with new formats or larger ring gauges.
- **Edición Limitada:** Given Trinidad's growing popularity, a limited edition from this brand would be met with great enthusiasm.
- **La Casa del Habano (LCDH) or Habanos Specialist Exclusives:** Trinidad is a strong contender for exclusive releases designated for these premium retailers.

### The Annual Staples: Edición Limitada and Regional Editions

Beyond the featured brands, 2026 will, of course, bring the usual highly anticipated releases:

- **Edición Limitada 2026:** Habanos S.A. typically announces three Edición Limitada cigars each year. These always feature tobaccos aged for at least two years and often come in unique sizes for their respective brands. We'll be waiting to see which three brands are chosen for these coveted releases.
- **Regional Editions (ERs):** Expect numerous regional editions tailored for specific markets around the globe. These offer unique vitolas for local distributors, often from less-globally prominent brands, and are highly sought after by collectors.

### The Unpredictables: Gran Reservas, Añejados, and Book Humidors

While not guaranteed every year for specific brands, 2026 could also see:

- **Gran Reserva or Reserva:** A new Gran Reserva (minimum 5 years aged filler, binder, wrapper) or Reserva (minimum 3 years aged filler, binder, wrapper) from a prominent brand. These are among the most luxurious and flavorful cigars.
- **Añejados:** More cigars from the "Añejados" series, which are regular production cigars that have been aged for 5-8 years *after* being rolled and boxed.
- **Colección Habanos (Book Humidor):** The next volume in this extremely limited and collectible series, which features a unique vitola presented in a wooden "book" box.

### Mark Your Calendars!

The XXVI Festival del Habano will be the moment when all these exciting plans are officially unveiled. Until then, the anticipation builds. One thing is certain: 2026 promises to be a monumental year for Cuban cigars, with Cohiba's 60th Anniversary leading the charge. Get ready to make some space in your humidors!
