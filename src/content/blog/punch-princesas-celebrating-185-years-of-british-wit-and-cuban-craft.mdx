The year 2025 marks a remarkable anniversary for one of Cuba's oldest and most characterful cigar brands: Punch. With a history stretching back an incredible 185 years, Habanos S.A. is paying homage to this venerable brand with a special release in the coveted Edición Limitada 2025 series: the **Punch Princesas**.

Punch cigars have long been cherished for their unique, medium-bodied profile, often delivering flavors of cedar, nuts, and a delightful underlying sweetness, distinct from other Cuban marques. The brand, originally created for the British market, has always exuded a certain playful sophistication, named after the famous English puppet Mr. Punch.

The **Punch Princesas** will feature a **52 ring gauge by 135 mm in length**, a versatile size that allows for a substantial smoking time without being overly imposing. As an Edición Limitada, the tobaccos used for the Princesas—wrapper, filler, and binder—have all undergone a minimum of two years of aging. This process is crucial for Punch, as it often softens any sharper edges and allows its nuanced flavors to meld beautifully, creating a smoother and more complex smoking experience.

Expect the Princesas to deliver a refined expression of the classic Punch profile, perhaps with enhanced notes of honey, spice, and the brand's signature underlying Cuban terroir. This is not just a limited edition; it’s a celebration of enduring legacy and an invitation to experience Punch at its aged finest.

Cigar enthusiasts and collectors should eagerly anticipate the arrival of the Punch Princesas. It's an opportunity to connect with a piece of cigar history and enjoy a beautifully crafted smoke that honors 185 years of Cuban tradition and British charm.
