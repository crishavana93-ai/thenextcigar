V-cuts are the cigar cutter you reach for when you smoke anything over 52 ring gauge — which means most modern cigars. Instead of taking the entire cap off, a V-cut creates a notch, concentrating the draw and letting more wrapper oils reach your palate. The smoke comes through richer, the cap stays intact, and the cigar doesn't unravel as you smoke it down.

The Colibri Premium V-Cut is the V-cut to buy. The spring-loaded stainless steel blade snaps closed with the kind of mechanical satisfaction that explains the 4.8-star rating across 5,200+ reviews — this is the most-reviewed premium cigar cutter on Amazon and almost certainly the most-owned V-cut in the modern aficionado's pocket. ## Maintenance — the part nobody talks about

A good cigar cutter, treated well, lasts a lifetime. A mistreated one dulls in 18 months. Three rules:

1. **Wipe the blade dry after every cut.** Tobacco oils corrode stainless steel over time. A cotton handkerchief is enough.
2. **Don't drop it.** Sounds obvious — most cutter failures come from a single drop that misaligns the blades.
3. **Send it back to the manufacturer when the edge dulls.** Xikar's lifetime warranty isn't theoretical. Send the cutter to their service center, they'll sharpen or replace it. Most owners never bother — but the option is there.
