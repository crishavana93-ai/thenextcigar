The consumable. The rare cigar accessory that's both repeat-purchase and immediately useful — every humidor benefits from fresh cedar every 6–12 months, and these sheets drop into any unit without modification.

**The wood:** Real kiln-dried Spanish cedar, cut into uniform sheets sized to fit standard humidor trays. Same wood that lines the best handmade humidors.

**The use:** Drop one or two sheets into an existing humidor when the cedar aroma starts to fade (usually 6–12 months in). The fresh wood re-aromatises the box and provides additional humidity buffering — Spanish cedar absorbs moisture at a slower, steadier rate than synthetic humidifiers, which is why every Habanos box uses it.
