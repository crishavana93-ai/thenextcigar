The triple-flame torch is the workhorse of cigar lighting — three blue flames combine into a single wide cone that lights an entire foot evenly. No re-rotating the cigar four times, no scorching one side. JOBON has been making butane cigar tools for over a decade, and this is their bread-and-butter design.

**Why three flames:**

- **Even foot light** — the cone width matches a 50-54-ring-gauge foot, the most common cigar size
- **Wind resistance** — three jets blocking each other means a 15 km/h breeze won't kill the flame
- **Visual draw confirmation** — you see the cigar fully alight before you take the first puff

**Features:**

- **Hidden punch cutter** — pop-out 8mm punch on the underside, so you don't need a backup tool when traveling
- **Visible gas window** — clear strip down the body, you know when to refill before the lighter dies on you
- **Adjustable flame** — wheel under the tank, range from candle-sized to blowtorch
- **Refillable butane** — standard premium butane refills, available at any tobacconist
