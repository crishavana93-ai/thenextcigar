**How 2-way packs work:**

They both release and absorb moisture, holding your humidor at the exact RH printed on the pack. If your humidor is at 70% and you've got a 65% pack inside, the pack absorbs moisture until the box drops to 65%. If the humidor falls to 60%, the pack releases moisture to bring it back up.

**Pick your RH:**

- **62%** — for long-term aging. Slows the rate of any chemical breakdown in the leaf.
- **65%** — standard storage. Most cigars are designed to be smoked at this RH.
- **69%** — the daily smoking RH. Cigars are ready to go directly from the humidor.
- **72%** — Cuban-style. Most appropriate for Cuban Habanos, which were rolled at higher humidity.
