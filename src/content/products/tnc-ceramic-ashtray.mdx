A working ashtray for the desk, the terrace, or the lounge corner you've claimed at home.

**The form:** 6-inch round, weighted base, twin cigar rests on opposite sides so two smokers can share without crowding the wells. Low-profile rim — no high lip that catches your wrist when you set the cigar down.

**The glaze:** Two options. Matte cream (the warmest piece on the table) or gloss charcoal (disappears into the desk surface, lets the cigar do the visual work).

**The branding:** TNC wordmark fired into the base, not the bowl. Invisible while in use, present when you wash it.

**Care:** Hand wash. Dishwasher won't kill it but it'll dull the gloss eventually.
