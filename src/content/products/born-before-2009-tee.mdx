The UK's [Tobacco and Vapes Act](/blog/uk-generational-tobacco-ban-becomes-law/) cuts the world's cigar buyers in two: born before 1 January 2009, and born after. If you were born before, this law leaves you alone — for life. If you were born after, you'll never legally buy a cigar in Britain.

This tee is the editorial response. A single line, set in our display serif, in cream or burgundy on heavyweight cotton. Worn at a lounge, it's the kind of quiet political tag that opens a fifteen-minute conversation with the only other person in the room who's read the bill.

**The print:** "BORN BEFORE 2009" set in 60-pt display serif across the chest. Small TNC mark on the back collar. Water-based ink with a soft hand — won't crack after twenty washes.

**The cotton:** 5.3 oz ringspun heavyweight, side-seamed, pre-shrunk. Cut a touch boxier than a slim fit so it sits cleanly under a jacket.

**Sizing:** True to size. Between two sizes? Size up.

**Care:** Cold wash, hang dry. Iron the print inside-out.
