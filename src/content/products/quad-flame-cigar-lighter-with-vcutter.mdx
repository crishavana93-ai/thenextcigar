The piece you keep in the desk drawer for when a friend turns up cigar-curious. Four jets give you faster, broader light than triple-flame — good for Toros and larger ring gauges. The V-cutter slides out the side of the body, so cutting and lighting happen with one tool in your hand.

**Why four flames:**

- **Wider light cone** — better for 56-ring-gauge and up, where triple flames need a second pass
- **Faster ignition** — half the time vs a single torch for the same cigar
- **Stays lit** — four jets reinforce each other against wind better than three
