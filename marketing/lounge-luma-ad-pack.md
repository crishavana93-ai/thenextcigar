# The Lounge — 5-Spot Luma Ad Pack

**Format:** 9:16 vertical, 8–12 sec each, organic-only (tobacco restrictions block paid)
**Posting cadence:** one spot per week × 5 weeks, in this order
**Pin-this-everywhere:** the brand sells the *community*, not the cigar

---

## The "dive into the phone" technique

Each spot ends with a transition INTO a phone screen showing The Lounge actually working. You can't fake this with Luma — it hallucinates UIs that look fake. Real flow:

1. **Luma generates the "world" footage** (4–6 sec) that ends with a phone visible in frame
2. **You screen-record the actual app on your phone** (4–6 sec) — the specific moment the overlay text is talking about (map filling with pins, a DM arriving, a trip declaration, etc.)
3. **You match-cut between them in CapCut** — at the frame where the Luma footage shows the phone screen biggest, jump cut to your screen recording. Result: viewer feels like they got pulled INTO the phone.

**iPhone screen-recording how-to:** Settings → Control Center → add Screen Recording. Then long-press the record button in Control Center → set audio source to Microphone if you want commentary, or off for silent. Recording starts after a 3-sec countdown. Files land in Photos.

**Post-production:** CapCut on phone. Free, fast, has built-in templates for "phone reveal" transitions. The whole compose takes ~15 min per spot once you've shot the screen recording.

## Spot 4 — "Cris, talking" (THE conversion driver)

**Run order:** Week 4. Most important spot. Highest expected CTR. **You film this on your phone, no Luma.**

### Setup

- Vertical iPhone, propped or held by a friend
- Evening warm light (lamp behind camera or window light)
- Dark wall or your bookshelf behind you — clean, no clutter
- You holding a lit cigar (smoke trail is the visual rhythm)
- No music, no edit fanciness, no transitions
- One single take, look directly at the lens

### Script (read it like you're talking to a friend, not pitching)

> "Look. I built The Lounge because I was tired of landing in Munich and not knowing a single cigar person there. If you've ever been the only guy at a cigar bar in a foreign city — you know exactly what I mean.
>
> It's free. It's at lounge.thenextcigar.com. Make me right."

**Length:** aim for 18–22 sec. If it runs over 25, re-record tighter.

### Optional B-roll cut (last 3 sec)

Hold the phone up to camera showing the app open on `/lounge/app/`. Lets viewers see what they're signing up to.

### Caption when posting

> Built it for a real problem I kept having. 25 members in. Founding 100 spots free for life. Comment "in" if you want me to send the link.

The "comment in" prompt is a known Instagram engagement hack. It also moves you toward members who actually asked.

## Style guide — overlay text rules

- **Two fonts only.** Headline = serif (Playfair Display or Cormorant). CTA = sans-serif (Inter or Helvetica Neue Medium). Both white or gold — never any other color.
- **Position:** top-third for problem statements, bottom-third for resolution lines. Center for CTAs only.
- **Two-second display minimum.** Less than that, viewers can't read it on mute.
- **No filters, no animations, no dust overlays.** Type fades in, holds, fades out. That's it.
- **Capitalize first word only.** "Tokyo. 11 p.m." not "TOKYO. 11 P.M."
- **One emotion per line.** Don't combine "you're alone AND we can fix it" — that's two lines.
