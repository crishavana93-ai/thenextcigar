## Engagement tactics

- **First-line hook**: opens before "...see more" cutoff (Instagram caps captions visible at ~125 chars before truncation)
- **Emoji as visual structure**: lists with 🇵🇹 / 🇷🇸 flag emojis pull eye-tracking down
- **Specific numbers**: "€1.80 to €3.75" beats "tax goes up"
- **Comment-bait CTA**: every post ends with a single question
- **Hashtag grouping**: 13-15 hashtags, separated by line breaks of `.` — keeps them visually separated from the caption body
- **One pinned comment** after posting: drop the blog link in the first comment ("Read the full breakdown: thenextcigar.com/blog/...") so the link stays sticky at the top of the comment thread
